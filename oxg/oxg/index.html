The most promising address in India’s most liveable city

Pune has always been popular for its culture and educational institutes. But in the recent past, it has come to be known for a lot more. And it’s not only the city’s IT industry. Locations like Bavdhan are attracting people in huge numbers by offering natural beauty as well as social infrastructure. So, let’s see what else makes Pune and Bavdhan such a favourite among homebuyers.

The Pune Advantage

  • Voted as India's most liveable city in Ease of Living index, 2018 released by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs
  • Declared as a Smart City by the Government of India Second biggest IT hub in India
  • Strong manufacturing base in Auto and Engineering Called the ‘Oxford of the East’ for its 811 colleges

The Bavdhan Advantage

  • Surrounded by Pashan Lake in north and hills on the other three sides, Bavdhan has seen gradual planned development
  • Proximity to Mumbai-Pune Express highway has sparked the area’s growth
  • Themed residences and high-end housing has been on a rise leading to appreciation in realty rates
  • Well poised to experience an appreciation above the rest of the market thanks to its location, infrastructure and connectivity
