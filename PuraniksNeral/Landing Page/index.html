Why Neral?

Neral is an extended suburb of Mumbai, falling under the Karjat Tehsil in Raigad district. Puraniks City Neral is connected to both - a buzzing city life and the tranquility of nature. It’s just a short drive away from two of the biggest cities of Maharashtra – Mumbai and Pune. Located at the foothills of Matheran, Neral is set to be the next big residential hub* in the state of Maharashtra.


Connectivity:-

Proposed projects that will certainly boost Neral’s real estate development:

  • 1. The Neral-Dasturi Naka-Matheran Road Project
  • 2. The development of the Badlapur-Karjat Road into a four-lane highway
  • 3. The Karjat-Murbad Road that will be developed as a part of the Virar-Murbad-Karjat-Khopoli-Alibaug Outer Ring Road
  • 4. A new state highway connecting Panvel and Neral via the Matheran hills
  • 5. A state highway connecting Panvel and Chakan via the Bhimashankar mountains, reducing travel distance by 21 km
  • 6. Easy access to industrial hubs nearby, mainly Kalyan and Badlapur
