Location

Metro

The phase 2 of metro will connect Gottigere to Nagawara and Phase 2B from Nagawara to Bengaluru International Airport..

STRR (Satellite Town Ring Road)

Proposed 6-Lane Satellite Town Ring Road will connect Airport Road Corridor with Doddaballapur, Tumkur, Hosur & Kanakapura.

Mall

One of India’s Largest Mall operator - Phoenix Market City has acquired land to develop market city in Airport Road Corridor

Office Spaces

Close to 15 Million sq.ft. of new office space is being added in the region which is expected to generate around 2 lakh new jobs.

SEZ

The KIADB has earmarked 1900 acres for development of Aerospace SEZ, IT & Hardware Park, and Devanahalli Business Park.

PRR (Peripheral Ring Road)

The Proposed 65 km Peripheral Ring Road will provide a faster alternative route to reach Airport Road Corridor from Electronic City, Sarjapur Road and KR Puram.
