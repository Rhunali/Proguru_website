Our Story


India has been witness to a sporting revolution. The country is awakening to the fact that to be able to give rise to award-winning sportsmen, we need to recognise and tap talent from a very early age. A major challenge faced in our society is the lack of quality sports infrastructure.
The objective of Royal Goldfieldd Club Resort is to provide world-class sports infrastructure. It is also our aim to encourage budding talent through sports camps, training academies and professional coaching. The Royal Goldfieldd Club Resort has been conceptualized on this principle foundation.
We plan to bring back yesteryear's sports tradition of playing amidst nature, where families with children can enjoy an active lifestyle. Similarly, we look forward to giving corporates a refreshing spin by regularly organising sports tournaments and team-building activities, thus nurturing better carmaraderic.

Whilst 'Sports for All' continues to be the nerve centre of our story, we will also enable an environment for outdoor adventure and create a leisure and lifestyle gateway near the sea and hills. This will be complimented through well-established club infrastructure and supported by world-class hospitality.
We are all set to give a new definition to family holidays in the beautiful landscape of Konkan.

  The Royal Goldfieldd Club Resort has already developed an English county-style pavilion and cricket ground, which will be further augmented by state-of-the-art infrastructure that will come up in phases.  

Dapoli

A Hat-Trick of Hills, Beaches & Forests

Dapoli is located 800 ft. above sea-level in the scenic Sahyadris, along the peaceful Konkan shoreline. The Royal Goldfieldd Club Resort is a unique Sports, Leisure & Lifestyle-Centric Club & Resort in Dapoli. The development is spread over a vast table-land of over 40 acres, overlooking the Anjarle, Harne and Murud beaches. This popular holiday and tourist destination has been declared a "Mini-Goa" for its untouched 50 km shoreline of beautiful beaches, and a "Mini-Mahabaleshwar" for its pleasant weather all year round.
